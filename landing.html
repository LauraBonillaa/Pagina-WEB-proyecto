What is Heaven's Official Blessing about?

Heaven Official’s Blessing is a story that follows the life of Xie Lian, an exiled prince who becomes a celestial official after a series of tragic events. The plot unfolds in a fantasy world inspired by Chinese mythology, where Xie Lian encounters various supernatural characters and becomes embroiled in divine intrigues. Central to the story is his relationship with Hua Cheng, an enigmatic being who accompanies and assists him in his adventures. Throughout the narrative, themes of love, redemption, loyalty and destiny are explored. The novel stands out for its rich setting, complex characters and captivating narrative style, attracting a wide fan base both in China and abroad.

Products

Donghua



It is a visually stunning adaptation that follows the story of Xie Lian and Hua Cheng in a fantasy world. It can be viewed on streaming platforms such as Tencent Video, crunchyroll and WeTV, among others, with subtitles available in several languages, including English.

Manhua

It is a comic book adaptation of the original novel. It features a visually appealing narrative that follows the plot of the main story. It can be read on online platforms such as Bilibili Comics, Tencent Comic and WebComics, among others, with reading options in several languages, including English.



Novels

It has approximately 8 novels, which are the beginning of the whole series and from these the manhua and donhua were created.
